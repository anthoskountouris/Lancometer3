What is Lancometer 3?

Lancometer 3 is online system that aims to deliver interactive quizzes and minigames in a remote, asynchronous , accessible and hopefully very fun manner! This project based on implementing exciting new features to the original software developed by Dr Daniel Finnegan which allowed to create quizzes that visually show the answers given by the users. Lancometer 3 will bring a game-based approach to quizzes, where students will be randomly assigned into teams who must compete to score points in minigames.

Frequently Asked Questions

As a student: You simply wait for your lecturer or teacher to give you the code to join their quiz, and login with that. For a more personalised experience, you can log in with your email and password.

As a Lecturer: It's a bit more complicated. You can find more information in your account page.

As a lecturer, hosting a quiz is simple. Collate your questions and answers into one file, specify who will have access to the quiz, and make a couple small tweaks in the settings and you're on your way. More detailed information can be found in your lecturer account page.

You can either join via code or by logging into the system yourself and accessing the quiz manually. Logging in will give a more personalised experience and may be required for certain quizzes

If joining only by code, you must also enter your email address, for security reasons.

Once you have logged into your account, you can access your account details such as your email and password, in addition to the function of deleting your account entirely.

We take your privacy very seriously, and give you full control over your data.

Our system complies with all GDPR principles. We are transparent about how we handle your data and only use your data when absolutely necessary for the function of the system.

If you move on from university and want to secure your data, you can select the option to retrieve or delete all the data we have saved about you from your account page.
